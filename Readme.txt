Prof. Amos Tai has given an R tutorial for fellow atmospheric scientists and written many functions useful for analyzing environmental, atmospheric, spatial and time series data. Please feel free to download them here (revised and updated periodically):Ê ¥ R tutorial for atmospheric scientistsÊ ¥ get_geo.R (functions for spatial analysis and visualization, e.g. interpolation, regridding, mapping, etc.)Ê ¥ get_met.R (functions for handling time series and meteorological data, e.g. moving average, monthly statistics, etc.)Ê ¥ get_stat.R (functions for statistical and numerical methods, e.g. optimization, regression, cumulative probability, etc.)Ê ¥ get_EPA.R (functions for handling EPA air quality datasets)
